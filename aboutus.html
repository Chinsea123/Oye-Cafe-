About Oye! Cafe

The newest cafe and dining restaurant in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, offering handcrafted food and beverages every day. Our cafe provides customers a modern and cozy atmosphere, which is a perfect place for you to relax and meet-up. Students, professionals, families, tourists and whoever that like delicious food would come here for a hangout spot.
A variety of items is available here, including coffee, tea, dessert, pastries, cakes, sandwiches, salad, main courses and more. The menu is designed specially for foodies that consists of different tastes and dietary preferences. Customers can expect an excellent dining experience that includes dishes prepared with premium ingredients that are freshly prepared.

~~~~~ Our Story ~~~~~

In February 2023, "Oye! Cafe" opened in a small storefront on Setapak Street. The idea of the name, Oye! Cafe comes from a group of four founders. The word "oye" is a common word used in South Asia, that means "hey" or "listen". With the name "Oye! Cafe", it conveys a sense of friendliness and informality, where we wish that people that visit our cafe get to relax and gather among friends.
We've started to plan the interior design of the cafe since last year in order to provide customers a comfortable and lovely place to enjoy their meal. In our cafe, we've prepared enough space for you to eat, gather, and work. All our furniture used is modern and aesthetic, with guaranteed quality.
The menu in Oye! Cafe has a variety of choices, from desert to main courses, from food to drinks, it surely satisfies everyone's taste. With self-taught chefs and willingness to cook, we invite you for all occasions, to celebrate, to gather, to chit-chat, to read and work, to have brunch and more...
